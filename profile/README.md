# FOSS PTU 🚀

**FOSS PTU** is a student-led open source community at **IK Gujral Punjab Technical University (IKGPTU)** and a local chapter of **FOSS United**.

Our mission is to build and sustain a strong **Free and Open Source Software (FOSS) culture** on campus.

> **From students, for students, and by students.**

---

## 🌱 What We Are

FOSS PTU is not just a student club — it is a **student-driven ecosystem for building and maintaining open technology**.

We encourage students to:

* Build and contribute to **real open source software**
* Maintain projects across **multiple student batches**
* Share knowledge and mentor others in the community

We believe students should **create technology, not just consume it.**

---

## 🛠 Our Work

Projects within this organization are **built and maintained by students of IKG-PTU**.

These projects may include:

* Developer tools
* Campus infrastructure software
* Community platforms
* Learning resources and documentation

Explore our work:

👉 [https://github.com/orgs/PTU-DEV/repositories](https://github.com/orgs/PTU-DEV/repositories)

## 🏫 Part of FOSS United

FOSS PTU operates as a local community under **FOSS United**, an initiative that promotes open source culture across India.

Learn more about our chapter:

👉 [https://fossunited.org/c/ik-gujral-punjab-technical-university](https://fossunited.org/c/ik-gujral-punjab-technical-university)

## 🤝 Get Involved

Students can contribute in many ways:

* Fixing issues (not limited to repositories in this organization)
* Improving the developer culture within PTU
* Maintaining and improving existing projects
* Proposing new ideas and building them together with the community

Open source grows through **participation, collaboration, and consistency.**

---

## 📜 Principles

Our community follows the core principles of FOSS:

* **Openness**
* **Collaboration**
* **Shared ownership**
* **Long-term sustainability**

Our aim is to build a **developer ecosystem at IKG-PTU that continues beyond individual batches.**
